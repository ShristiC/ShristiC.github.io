Project information

  • Category: Cross-Platform App Development
  • Purpose: Help field workers in India provide COVID-19 relief by streamlining data retrieval
  • Project date: July - August 2020

Our App

I developed this app along with my friend Victoria, and together we made implemented a simple design encompassing the core funcitonalities of the app. The app's core functionality is to sign a user in, display a form in which the user must input details regarding the client. Then upon submitting the form, they have the option to fill out another form for a different client. The front-end of the app was developed on flutter, and the back-end entails using Firebase and Google Authentication. The app is in the process of being launched to the Google Play Store for immediate use, and will hopefully be launched on the IOS app store. DEMO VIDEO COMING SOON!
